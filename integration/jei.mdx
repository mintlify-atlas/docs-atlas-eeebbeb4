Create: Simulated registers all of its recipes with Just Enough Items (JEI). If you have JEI installed, you can look up crafting inputs, outputs, and processing steps for every item in the mod without leaving the game.

## Requirements

You need **JEI 19.21.0.247 or later**. Earlier versions do not support the recipe categories that Create: Simulated registers.

## Looking up recipes

Press **R** while hovering over any Create: Simulated item in your inventory or in the JEI item list to see all recipes that produce it. Press **U** to see all recipes that consume it.

This works for every item in the mod, including multi-step crafting chains involving Create machinery such as the Mechanical Press, Mixing, and Compacting.

## Portable Engine dyeing

The Portable Engine supports 16 dye colour variants. The dyeing recipe — crafting a Portable Engine together with any vanilla dye — is visible in JEI under the standard crafting table category.

To find it:

1. Open JEI and search for `Portable Engine`.
2. Press **R** on the item to open its recipe list.
3. Scroll through the recipe pages to find the dyeing variants.

Each colour variant appears as a separate recipe entry, one per dye colour.

<Tip>
You can also hover over a dye in your inventory and press **U** to see all items that accept that dye, including the Portable Engine.
</Tip>

## Recipe categories

JEI groups Create: Simulated recipes into the same categories Create uses:

| Category | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Crafting | Shaped and shapeless crafting table recipes. |
| Compacting | Recipes processed in the Mechanical Press. |
| Mixing | Recipes processed in the Mechanical Mixer. |
| Deploying | Recipes using the Mechanical Deployer. |

All categories appear in the JEI recipe browser alongside standard Create recipes, so you can cross-reference machine chains in one place.
