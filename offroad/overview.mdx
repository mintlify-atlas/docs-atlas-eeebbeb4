The Offroad module brings ground transportation and tunnel drilling to Create: Simulated. You can build wheel-driven land vehicles using Wheel Mounts and tires in four sizes, cut through stone with the Rock Cutting Wheel, and drill entire tunnels forward using the Borehead Bearing.

## Key blocks and items

| Block / Item | Function |
|---|---|
| **Wheel Mount** | Holds a tire and provides drive and steering to the contraption |
| **Small Tire** | Smallest tire (1-block); low mass, low clearance |
| **Tire** | Standard all-terrain tire |
| **Large Tire** | Belt-based large tire; higher clearance |
| **Monstrous Tire** | Largest tire, dried kelp block-based; maximum clearance and traction |
| **Rock Cutting Wheel** | Spinning cutting block that breaks stone and rock in its path |
| **Borehead Bearing** | Drills the contraption linearly forward, mining a tunnel |

<Tip>
  Press **W** while looking at any Offroad block in-game to open its ponder scene for a visual walkthrough.
</Tip>
