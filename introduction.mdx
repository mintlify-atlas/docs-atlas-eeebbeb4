Create: Simulated is a suite of NeoForge mods that extends the [Create](https://modrinth.com/mod/create) mod with real physics simulation. Using the three modules — **Simulated**, **Aeronautics**, and **Offroad** — you can assemble contraptions that fly through the air on hot air balloons, propellers, or anti-gravity Levitite, or drive across terrain on tire-equipped land vehicles.

## Key concepts

- **Simulated Contraptions** are physics-enabled assemblies created by the Physics Assembler. Unlike standard Create contraptions, they interact with gravity, air pressure, and physical forces.
- **Blocks and components** from all three modules attach to a Simulated Contraption to give it thrust, steering, lift, or sensor data.
- **ComputerCraft peripherals** are available for most sensor and control blocks, enabling full Lua-based automation of your vehicles.

<Note>
  Create: Simulated requires **NeoForge 21.1.219+** and **Minecraft 1.21.1**. The Create mod version **6.0.9** is also required as a dependency.
</Note>
