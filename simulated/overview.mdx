The Simulated module is the foundation of Create: Simulated. It introduces the Physics Assembler — the block you use to convert any glued structure into a freely-moving, physics-simulated contraption — alongside a full set of kinetic control blocks, environmental sensors, redstone automation components, and utility items.

## What the Simulated module provides

### Physics assembly

The **Physics Assembler** is the central block of the entire mod. You attach it to any group of blocks held together with Super Glue or Honey Glue, hold right-click, and pull the lever to launch them as a fully physics-simulated contraption. The contraption obeys gravity, collides with terrain, and remains fully interactable — you can open chests, flip levers, and ride it.

### Kinetics and control

Simulated contraptions need power and steering. The Simulated module provides:

- **Portable Engine** — a self-contained fuel-burning stress source in 16 dye colors that replaces Create's network-dependent generators on a moving contraption.
- **Swivel Bearing** — assembles a group of blocks as a sub-contraption that can rotate relative to the parent, similar to a Mechanical Bearing but for physics objects.
- **Steering Wheel and Throttle Lever** — player-operated inputs that drive Portable Engine output and bearing angles.
- **Directional Gearshift and Analog Transmission** — redstone-controlled kinetic routing blocks.
- **Rope Winch and Rope Connector** — tether one contraption to another or to the world, and reel rope in or out with rotational force.
- **Torsion Spring, Auger Shaft, Auger Cogwheel** — specialized kinetics for spring-return motion and item extraction from harvesting actors.

### Sensors

Six sensor blocks report environmental data as a redstone signal, and each exposes a ComputerCraft peripheral for Lua-based automation:

- **Altitude Sensor** — proportional signal based on Y-level and air pressure.
- **Velocity Sensor** — proportional signal based on contraption speed along a configurable axis.
- **Optical Sensor** — detects blocks in its line of sight and reports distance.
- **Laser Pointer + Laser Sensor** — beam-based sensing pair; the sensor emits a signal when the beam is unobstructed.
- **Gimbal Sensor** — emits a signal proportional to the contraption's tilt on the X and Z axes.
- **Navigation Table** — outputs a directional redstone signal toward a target set by a lodestone compass or other navigation item.

### Redstone automation

Blocks such as the **Redstone Accumulator**, **Redstone Inductor**, **Docking Connector**, **Modulating Linked Receiver**, and **Linked Typewriter** give you fine-grained control over signals across moving contraptions and wireless links.

### Items

Utility items — **Spring**, **Rope Coupling**, **Honey Glue**, **Merging Glue**, **Contraption Diagram**, **Plunger Launcher**, and the creative-mode **Creative Physics Staff** — support assembly, tethering, merging contraptions, and debugging.
